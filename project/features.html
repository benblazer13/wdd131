Powerful Features for Smarter Shopping

Everything you need to plan, shop, and save with confidence

Smart Shopping Lists

Create and manage multiple shopping lists with intelligent features that learn from your shopping patterns. Items are automatically organized by store section, making your shopping trips faster and more efficient.

  • Automatic categorization by store aisle
  • Predictive text suggests frequently bought items
  • Check off items as you shop
  • Add custom notes to any item
  • Duplicate lists for recurring shopping trips

Meal Planning Integration

Plan your meals for the week and automatically generate shopping lists with all required ingredients. Save your favorite recipes and meal plans for future use.

  • Weekly meal calendar view
  • Recipe storage with ingredient auto-extraction
  • Serving size adjustments
  • Nutritional information tracking
  • One-click shopping list generation from meal plans

Budget Tracking & Analytics

Set monthly grocery budgets and track your spending in real-time. Get insights into where your money goes and discover opportunities to save.

  • Visual spending charts and graphs
  • Category-based budget allocation
  • Price comparison history
  • Spending alerts when approaching limits
  • Export reports for personal finance apps

Smart Reminders

Never forget to pick up groceries again. Get location-based reminders when you're near your favorite stores, or set time-based alerts for when items need to be restocked.

  • Location-aware notifications
  • Custom reminder schedules
  • Low-stock alerts for pantry items
  • Recurring reminder patterns
  • Do Not Disturb mode for focus times

Shopping Insights

Understand your shopping habits with detailed analytics. See trends, identify savings opportunities, and reduce food waste with smart insights.

  • Monthly shopping pattern analysis
  • Most frequently purchased items
  • Price trend tracking
  • Waste reduction suggestions
  • Store comparison data

Family Sharing

Collaborate with family members on shopping lists and meal plans. See real-time updates as others add or check off items.

  • Invite family members via email or link
  • Real-time synchronization
  • Assign shopping tasks to family members
  • Comment on items with family notes
  • Privacy controls for shared lists
