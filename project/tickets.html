Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all tickets within 1-2 business days. Critical bug reports affecting app functionality are prioritized and addressed within 24 hours.

Can I track the status of my ticket?

Yes! After submitting a ticket, you'll receive a ticket number. Your recent submissions are also shown above for quick reference. We'll email you updates as your ticket is processed.

What information should I include in a bug report?

Please include: steps to reproduce the bug, what you expected to happen, what actually happened, your device type, and app version. Screenshots are very helpful!

How do I submit a feature request?

Select "Feature Request" as the ticket type and describe the feature you'd like to see. Explain the problem it would solve and how you envision using it. The more detail, the better!
